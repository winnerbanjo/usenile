💡 Why most brands choose Growth

Growth gives you the best balance between price and value. You get your own custom domain, unlimited products, unlimited orders, abandoned cart reminders, and better tools to follow up with customers.

It is the best option for brands that want to look professional and grow beyond just Instagram or WhatsApp selling.

Important Notes

  • • All plans last for 3 months.
  • • Your Nile sub-domain is free.
  • • Custom domains are free for the first year only on Growth and Premium.
  • • After the first year, domain renewal will be paid separately.
  • • Store setup includes product, payment, and delivery configuration.
  • • You can manage your store from the Nile dashboard.
  • • You can upgrade your plan as your business grows.
